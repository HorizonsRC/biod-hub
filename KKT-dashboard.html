Kanorau Koiora Taketake
Indigenous Biodiversity Contestable Fund

Supporting locally-led conservation projects across Manawatū-Whanganui. Open annually in April/May, grants fund activities with clear community involvement and biodiversity benefit — assessed by a Councillor panel and supported by Biodiversity staff throughout delivery.

Koitiata Wetlands Restoration Group — 34 events in one year, 500+ volunteer hours
Castlecliff Coast Care — 10–20 events/year engaging up to 40 volunteers
Ihaia Taueki Trust — grew from 30 to 70 whānau members planting in consecutive years
Ruahine Whio Protectors — 19 whio released through fund support
