Activity Summary
Predator Trapping
8,625 trap checks by 2 contractors
Regular predator trapping carried out in the Retaruke and Whanganui River Catchments for whio protection, and in the Waimarino area for kiwi protection. Some checks delayed by weather events (high river levels, track closures) cleared by late December.
