Bushy Park Tarapuruhi

An 89 ha predator-proof forest sanctuary northwest of Whanganui, protecting a rare remnant of temperate lowland bush effectively free of pest mammals since 2005. Home to reintroduced hihi, tīeke, toutouwai, popokatea and tītipounamu.
