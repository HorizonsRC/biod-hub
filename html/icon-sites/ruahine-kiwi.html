Activity Summary
Predator Control
Large-scale predator control programme protecting kiwi in the Ruahine Range. Network of traps and bait stations targeting rats, possums, mustelids, and hedgehogs.

HRC Support
Annual grant funding from 2024–25 following Long-term Plan submission. HRC also manages PCO zones in the adjacent Ruahine landscape (see results above).
Governance
Currently operating under ENM's auspices while working towards establishing a standalone legal entity. A governance group including iwi and community stakeholders meets every two months.
