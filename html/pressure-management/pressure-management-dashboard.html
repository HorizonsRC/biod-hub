Environmental Pressures at Priority Habitat Sites

Pressure management is measured across 96 Priority Habitat sites by scoring how well six key environmental pressures are being managed: ungulates, pest plants, possum browse, predation, environmental disturbance, and rabbits/hares. Each site receives an overall score (0–100%).

Above 55% — Adequate management, ecosystems likely to improve
Below 55% — Greater management effort required to prevent degradation
Scoring Criteria by Pressure Type
