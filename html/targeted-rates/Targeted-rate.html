Targeted Rate Project
Waitārere Beach Community Project

The Waitārere Beach community pays a targeted rate to Horizons to control exotic plants and restore native plants on the dunes. This programme has focused on environmental restoration in areas along the dunes, residential beach frontage, and the banks of the Wairarawa Stream from the beach upstream to Wairarawa Lagoon.

Location Waitārere Beach, Horowhenua
Focus Exotic Plant Control & Dune Restoration
Key Corridor Wairarawa Stream to Lagoon
Reporting Period 2024–25 Financial Year
Targeted Rate Project
Rangitīkei Environment Group

Rangitīkei residents pay a targeted rate to Horizons for the control of Old Man's Beard in their district. The Rangitīkei Environment Group carries out this control work. The group was founded in 2001, and its primary goal is to address the infestation of Old Man's Beard in the Rangitīkei area.

Location Rangitīkei District
Focus Old Man's Beard Control
Group Established 2001
Reporting Period 2024–25 Financial Year
